Ask Louie — Jan 27 2026
✕
Hi! I'm Louie. Ask me anything about the Road Safety Committee meeting on January 27, 2026.
What was decided about the $2.2M?
The committee decided to deploy the $2.2M equitably across all 11 wards — approximately $200K each — focused on physical traffic calming in school zones. The plan goes to General Committee next month, with construction starting in 2026. The March 2028 deadline applies.

Which questions have never been resolved?
